Explanations:
CPA – cost to acquire one new customer.
Fixed costs – daily fixed expenses (excl. VAT).
Daily customers – number of new customers per day.
Purchase/Selling price – gross prices incl. VAT, converted internally to net.
% buy 2 pairs – percentage of customers who buy 2 pairs initially.
% voucher redeemers – percentage of 2-pair buyers who return with a voucher.
Voucher amount – discount value applied on the 3rd pair.
Avg profit/client – average profit per customer (before/after CPA & fixed).
POAS – Profit Over Ad Spend (profit/CPA). >1 = profitable.
Per segment – profit for 1-pair, 2-pair, and voucher customers.
Daily Projection – simulation of daily and monthly profit.
Customer Breakdown – distribution of customers per 100 and gross total profit.
Break-even/day – how many customers/day are needed to cover CPA + fixed costs.
